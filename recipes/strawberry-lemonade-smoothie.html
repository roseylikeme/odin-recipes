Strawberry Lemonade Smoothie

Description

Quick and easy refreshing smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Lemonade
  • 3/4 cup lemon yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups frozen unsweetened strawberries

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender; cover and process 15 seconds or until blended.
  2. Serve immediately.
